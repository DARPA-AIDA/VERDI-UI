Why are Animal Charities Important?

  •      The spay and neuter programs offered by dog shelters can be the most humane way to reduce the country's number of neglected animals.
  •    Dog shelters usually provide informative education programs for adults and children to know more about caring for animals.
  •    Pets also get affected when natural disaster strikes.

    •     As of 2020, more than 35 million stray dogs live in the streets of India.The number is expected to increase as more households decided to abandon their pets due to the pandemic.These vulnerable pets require proper care and nourishment to survive comfortably.

       These dogs can get the help and protection they need from animal charities, but people are encouraged to donate to dog shelters to help these organizations afloat.Animal shelters and rescue missions for abandoned and abused pets need regular donations from the community to continue their operations.

       Most dog shelters in India do not have enough resources to care for and feed all the animals under their care.It forces them to euthanize most dogs if they cannot find ways to generate resources, which is why donations for these organizations are badly needed.

        #1: Animal Shelters Help Prevent The Rising Population Of Unwanted Puppies And Kittens

       The spay and neuter programs offered by dog shelters can be the most humane way to reduce the country’s number of neglected animals.It can help the neighborhood manage free-roaming dogs that may pass on various diseases or cause various accidents and animal bites.

       If you choose to donate to a dog shelter, you will be able to help the organization launch an aggressive effort to spay and neuter stray dogs brought into their facility.With the help of these programs, the animal shelters will be able to curb the increasing number of puppies on the streets.These puppies will most likely mature and get old even without a family to care for them, which means they can also reproduce and add more strays.

       #2: Animal Shelters Educate The Society

       Dog shelters usually provide informative education programs for adults and children to know more about caring for animals.These programs aim to let people know the benefits of adopting animals instead of shopping for pets.

       With proper education, more people will understand their responsibilities as pet owners.It will also teach them how to take care of their dogs to prevent them from straying.Most importantly, learning more about pet ownership will let everyone know about helping animals in the community.

       #3: Animal Shelters Forge Unbreakable Bonds Between People And Dog

       Dogs can provide unconditional love to anyone who takes care of them properly.If you visit one of the dog shelters in your community, you will see a number of dogs who are willing to shower you with love if you choose to give them a chance.

       Animal shelters can help you adopt a new pet anytime you are ready to take one at home.They can help prepare the dogs for their new living conditions.The shelters can also use their resources to ensure that the dogs are safe to mingle with their new family and environment to avoid unwanted experiences.

        #4: Animal Shelters Serve As Haven For Pets After A Disaster

       Pets also get affected when natural disaster strikes.Plenty of pets get displaced after a hurricane, earthquake, massive flood, or any other type of catastrophes.These organizations will do everything to ensure that dogs and other animals are safe and can return to their owners immediately once everything is settled.

       Since dog shelters also get affected by these disasters, they also need the community’s support to allow them to rescue and care for animals.It means they need donations of any form to continue their services to the animals in the community.

       #5: Animal Shelters Help Improve The Health And Well-being Of The Animals And The Community

       If a shelter has all the resources needed to care for multiple animals, it can provide optimal veterinary support to all pets that they handle.They can also provide more affordable veterinary care to those who will adopt from their facility.

       It will help prevent the rapid spread of diseases that usually affect animals.Also, providing adequate veterinary care to stray animals will help curb the spread of illnesses from pets that they can transfer to humans.It means that everyone in the neighborhood will be protected from serious diseases like rabies.

       Bonus Reason: Tax Benefits

       Based on Indian bylaws, any donations to dog shelters or other nonprofit organizations through direct bank transfers of online transactions can get the 80G tax exemption.

       Donating to a dog shelter in India has plenty of perks for the donors.But the best thing about it is your contribution to help an organization raise and care for many stray animals can help solve the growing number of abandoned and neglected dogs on the streets.You will also help ensure that these animals are properly fed and loved.
