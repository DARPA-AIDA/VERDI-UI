Are Trump supporters now questioning their president?

    Following the news that Trump has tested positive for COVID 19, his supporters are now likely questioning what Trump has been saying about the virus for months.

   He has refused to wear a mask, mocked Joe Biden for wearing one and has blatantly refused to encourage his country to take precautions during this pandemic.

   Instead he has encouraged people to live their lives as normal and proactively promoted social gatherings by holding his rallies without taking potentially life saving precautions.

   With news emerging that Trump is now suffering mild symptoms, which could possibly turn severe due to his age and weight, is Donald Trump now suffering the direct consequences of his own ignorance and neglect against a virus which does not discriminate anyone.Including the President of the USA.

   So what do his devoted supported now do.The ones who believed everything he said regarding the corona virus.

   Will they change their minds about the whole thing and realize that COVID wasn’t a hoax after all?Or will they believe, like others, that Donald Trump doesn’t actually have COVID but instead this is a staged event to yet again divert from where the real stories lie.Trumps taxes for example.

   Only time will tell, but this may change the course of things to come and the spread of COVID if Trump really does have COVID and if he suffers from severe symptoms.We may actually people start to wear masks when they aren’t social distancing.We may see more people taking more precautions when socializing, or even decide to not socialize at all.

   Either way, the news that Donald Trump has COVID 19 is going to have knock on effects in either a really positive way, or a completely devastating way.
