    •     Shopify has proved to be one of the easiest and the best platform to launch an online business.There was a time when businesses would have to hire developers to build their website from scratch and add in enough functionality to make it good enough.However, those times have changed.

       Now eCommerce business owners just need to visit Shopify to create their business and make it visible to the rest of the world.Although using Shopify is very easy, building a website on the platform can be a little daunting.However, the good thing is that it is a very easy platform to handle and you can easily make your website there.

       Here is a guide for you to start your Shopify store and make the best of it before you start taking Shopify SEO services for maximum results.

        Start with the Free Trial

       When you sign up with Shopify, you get to have a 14-day free trial where you get to experience the platform before making any commitments.All new users get a free trial at this platform so that you can properly prepare your store and have it ready when the launch date comes.To start the trial, all you have to do is enter your email address, a password, and the name of your store.

       Setup Your Store

       You will then need to set up your store and provide basic information.By clicking on the General tab in the settings, you can add more details about your store, set up a currency, and even add a support email for your customers.You will also add in the payment methods your customers can use to pay you for your products.Finally, you will also have to select the appropriate shipping method.You can offer free shipping across the globe by adjusting your prices or you can adjust the shipping zones added by Shopify and only select the regions where you want to ship your products at the moment.

        Buy Your Domain

       After this basic information is provided, you need to purchase a domain and if you already have it, you need to connect it with your Shopify store.You will find the domain tab under the online store option in your dashboard.Click on the domain option, choose to purchase a new one, or transfer your already bought domain by following the on-screen instructions.

       Choose Your Themes

       You need to choose a nice theme that goes with your store’s aesthetics and your brand logo.There are many free themes available in Shopify and if none of them meets your requirements, you can always purchase them at Shopify as well.Of course, you also have the option to customize a theme and make sure you get the theme to look like exactly how you want it.

       After that, it is all smooth sailing.You just need to add new pages that are most important to your business add your products and make sure have the right pictures to see your products.Once you are done, you are ready to start your business.
