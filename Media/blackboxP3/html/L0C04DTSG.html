What to Consider When Moving Interstate in Australia

    •     According to the Australian Bureau of Statistics, during 2017-2018, almost 393,000 people moved interstate, up by 4.3% from the previous year.Further, about 43% of Australians moved their home in the past 5 years.So, moving interstate is a likely decision for most of Australia’s population.Nonetheless, whether you’re moving for lifestyle or work, just like the states vary, so do the challenges you’ll face when moving.Either way, here’s a list of the top 5 tips to guarantee a seamless relocation experience whenever you decide to change the scenery in Australia.

       Draft Your Checklist

       You should draw up a holistic checklist and include performing the necessary procedures to inform the government authorities about your change of address, the banks and utility or service providers you have contracts with.You can send out most notifications online.Also, if you are on a specific visa, it may require that you inform the Immigration Department of your new address.Besides that, Medicare also must know your new residential address.You could also leave a forwarding address from your old home to make sure you receive all your mail until the changes are fully recorded with different authorities and service providers.

       Verify the Quarantine Legislation

       Australia has strict quarantine rules and regulations, which are different from state to state.So, what’s legal in one state, could be illegal in another.Before you take the next step, study the quarantine laws applicable in the state where you’re relocating.Or, at least, don’t take any plants, agricultural equipment or animal products, which may contain soil or contaminants.To be sure you’re abiding by the specific rules, check the government’s quarantine website for more details.

       Don’t Take Useless Items with You

       You can simplify moving to another house by throwing away any products and items that you no longer need.Decluttering will keep all moving costs on the low.To get rid of any old objects or useless items, you can organize a garage sale or sell them online.It will also help you with packaging as the volume of items, which you’ll need to pack will decrease.And to make everything easier, considering local home removalists in Brisbane like Gantic Removals is another option.They provide fully integrated relocation services including packing, furniture and equipment removals, transportation and unpacking.

       How Will You Travel?

       Depending on your budget, you should decide the best way to travel to your new home.If you want to go by car, you should consider the extra mileage you’ll clock and the price of fuel.Instead, you could hire specialists to transport your vehicle.If your children are young and you also have pets, your best choice would be to travel by plane.Travelling by air is not only more comfortable but it also saves time.

       Insure Any Valuable Products

       Moving interstate is a complex procedure and you’re better off insuring your valuable items in case of any incidents.Even if you plan every little detail, an unexpected event could occur and you could suffer damages.Your best approach is to discuss the matter with Australian removalists who can provide the best guidance and help you choose the right option.

       The Bottom Line

       Moving to another state in Australia is a difficult process if you don’t have the right help.So, depending on your requirements, you should contact the removalists to provide the necessary help.

       Moving interstate in Australia doesn’t have to be daunting.So, take the right approach and make your move as easy as possible.Study and follow your checklist and, step by step, tick off every item you’ve included from start to finish.
