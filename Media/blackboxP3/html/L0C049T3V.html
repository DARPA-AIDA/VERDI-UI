One America News Network and AWE Secure Continued Broad Distribution on Buckeye Broadband and MaxxSouth Broadband

    FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

   Source: Herring Networks, Inc.

   San Diego, CA – September 17, 2020, Herring Networks, Inc., announced today that

   One America News Network   , ('

   OAN   ')   and A Wealth of Entertainment ('AWE') have reached a long-term distribution agreement with Buckeye Broadband and MaxxSouth Broadband for continued broad distribution of One America News Network and AWE.

   'We appreciate our partnership with Buckeye and MaxxSouth and are thrilled that Buckeye and MaxxSouth Broadband customers will have continued access to OAN and AWE.One America News’ appeal is more live, credible national and international news throughout the day with less talking heads expressing opinions, while AWE offers vicarious living and aspirational lifestyle and entertainment programming with broad appeal.'said Ann Schick, Executive Vice President Content Distribution of Herring Networks.

   'We are pleased to continue our relationship and that our customers will continue to have access to both OAN and AWE for 24/7 news and lifestyle and entertainment programming', said Geoff Shook, President and General Manager of Buckeye Broadband.

   According to third party set top box data from a major MVPD for the week of 8/24, OAN was the 8

   th   ranked national cable network for all genres and was the 4

   th   ranked national news network.AWE ranked 95th for all genres (excluding broadcasters) and is position in the top third of all channels measured.During the RNC National Convention, viewers turned to OAN in mass for live coverage, driving OAN’s share rating to 2.609 during the 9 pm hour.

   In addition to Buckeye Broadband and MaxxSouth Broadband, One America News Network and AWE are broadly distributed by many distribution partners across the United States, the Caribbean, Canada, and the Middle East.Please visit www.oann.com/wheretowatch for a complete list of how to watch.
