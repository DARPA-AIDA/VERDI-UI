Why UGC Can Be the Perfect eCommerce Content Marketing Tool

  •      UGC is a cost-effective way of content marketing as the users create the content.
  •    The search engine loves content that is relevant and beneficial to its users.
  •    All eCommerce businesses are ultimately looking for marketing strategies that can increase the conversion rate and sales.

    •     Time after time, User-Generated Content has proven to be the most authentic, genuine, and trustworthy form of content.UGC is the piece of content created by the loyal users of brands that can surely take the business of the brand ahead and maximize their profits.

       E-Commerce businesses are constantly on a hunt to increase their brand presence.Social and social media acts as a perfect platform to gather User-Generated Content since it is a massive platform with billions of daily active users.

       Users leverage social media as a platform to openly share their opinions about new products and services.There is a chance that they are talking about your eCommerce brand.

        Hence, as an eCommerce brand, you must indeed utilize the power of User-Generated Content as a marketing tool to boost your brand conversions and sales.By now, you must be wondering why?In this blog, we will be answering this in detail.Keep reading!

       User-Generated Content – The Perfect Marketing Tool To Increase Your E-Commerce Sales

        Cost-Effective Yet Powerful

       UGC is a cost-effective way of content marketing as the users create the content.Save the extra penny that you would shell out to collaborate with a famous influencer on social media or various other advertising means.

       User-Generated Content is super powerful since it is created by users who adore your brand.Be it in the form of images, videos, gifs, Google or Facebook reviews; this content can prove to be beneficial for your brand; as a person tends to trust another person much more than your brand’s published content!

       Increased SEO Ranking

       The first step which people do while looking for a product is to search for it online.They instantly go to the search engine and look for Google reviews, video testimonials, etc.To put their faith into a brand is all they want and are looking for.

       The search engine loves content that is relevant and beneficial to its users.The more the users engage with your brand and increase traffic on your website, the higher you will rank on the local SEO.

       Moreover, UGC consists of the keywords that people generally search for, and hence it improves your discoverability.

       Build A Solid Social Proof

       Consider this – you are looking for a salon service near your locality.You google the ‘best salons in your area’.Most of us would do that, right?

       You see a list of best salons near you and immediately go through the reviews.You will shortlist the salon with the best reviews and take their services when you read positive reviews from other customers about their services.

       Eye-catching pictures and fancy descriptions of a salon without any review would not have been convincing enough for you to take up their services.

       This is the power of creating social proof.By doing this, you can gain the trust of your existing as well as potential customers.

       Increased Conversions Sales

       All eCommerce businesses are ultimately looking for marketing strategies that can increase the conversion rate and sales.You can leverage platforms like Taggbox commerce to create shoppable UGC galleries.

       By displaying User-Generated Content, you encourage and inspire regular shoppers to become your customers.As mentioned above, all shoppers look for is brand credibility and positive reviews.

        Improved Shopping Experience

       Gone are the days when shoppers used to rely on the brand’s content.The key to remember is not to underestimate your shoppers.They are smart and always look for what other shoppers experience.

       When they see the content posted by other customers using your products or services, they visualize the products on themselves too and are more likely to be satisfied with the quality of the product, further resulting in a better shopping experience.

       UGC- The Future Of Content Marketing

       Customers are the king, and without them, your business cannot thrive and survive.Your business becomes successful once you build a relationship with your customers on a personal level.

       Everybody likes to get featured.Show your customers that you value their feedback and encourage them to give reviews.If a customer tags your brand in a post, repost it on your feed and tag them.

       This will give them and your other customers the motivation to engage more with your brand.

       Moreover, user-generated content provides visibility to your eCommerce brand that no other paid marketing campaigns can.

       Over To You

       User-generated content is the most powerful and result-driven form of content marketing, which can be a treasure for eCommerce brands if leveraged properly.

       Using UGC can improve your engagement with your users, increase your brand value and visibility, build social proof, instill faith in your existing and potential customers, gradually increase your brand conversion rate and boost your sales.

       However, to enjoy all the mentioned benefits, you need a highly responsive and user-friendly social media aggregator which can effectively collect, curate, and showcase your UGC on your website!

    •     For a contractor, your tools are as necessary as any other part of the business since you use them to deliver services to your clients.Imagine how your career and life, in general, would be without them!Jobs wouldn’t be completed, work would be harder to do, and you would end up losing clients now and then.However, there is a thin line between having tools and having suitable tools.Therefore, to make it in this highly competitive business, you got to have the right tools that effectively get the job done.

       Below are some of the most important tools that you should have as a contractor.

        1.Tape Measure

       There are not many tools more essential to a contractor than a tape measure.At the end of the day, your job requires the highest precision levels to get the appropriate measurements.When you use this measuring resource correctly, you will consistently deliver the fastest and most accurate means of guaranteeing a successful building operation.

       As a contractor, you should know that it is paramount to ensure that every measurement taken is accurate; position, height, length, and even width.The good thing is that as technology advances and innovations surfacing now and then, tape measures and other measuring tools are becoming more specialized and precise.

       There are different types of tape measures.The most common one is the short tape measure with a small stiff tape used to take measurements up to forty feet.You can easily tuck one into your pocket or work bag for quick access when needed.

       The other common type of tape measure is a diameter tape measure.This makes it easier to measure around an object, a hard task for the stiff tape measure.It will give you an accurate circumference, measuring up to 6 feet.

       2.Level

       A contractor uses a level in a construction field when they want to set up a level point or check the surface’s elevation.Optically used, the tool is mainly used in surveying and construction.It can also e used to measure horizontal levels, for example, before setting a shoring jack to ensure that the surface is flat or elevated as required.

       The good thing about a contractor’s level is that buying it does not require you to break the bank.Moreover, the level is also easy to assemble and use.Its lightweight nature makes it easy to carry it around.

       One might wonder, is leveling really important in architectural works?Yes, it is.For you to come up with a successful building or surface, you must get the levels right.Gravity is the most significant force that undermines durability and functioning.Therefore, you have to work parallel and perpendicular to this force for the best results.Additionally, straight and level surfaces improve the aesthetic value of the surface.

        3.Adjustable Wrench

       One of the greatest challenges that a contractor goes through is carrying around their bulky material from one site to another.Gone are the days when a contractor would carry around a variety of wrenches for all their contractor jobs.The ability of a contractor to exclude any bulk is crucial for their performance.Weight will always hold you back.Today, you can get an

       An adjustable wrench that saves you from this hassle.Adding it to your arsenal not only eliminates the bulk from the tool belt and box it also provides an easier way to apply torque and turn nuts and bolts.

       Conclusion

       A contractor’s toolbox can never be complete without the adjustable wrench.What are the uses of this crucial tool?

  •     Assembling furniture.When you buy furniture, it comes as pieces that you have to join together to form one complete piece, for example, a bed.

  •     Plumbing and car repairs

    •      Yes, an adjustable wrench may not save you from all contractor problems.However, their set of uses can be used in different scenarios, not just construction.

       Tools make work easier.With a complete set of them, a contractor is able to get the job done within a flash.The right selection makes the difference between a successful contactor and a basic one.Since it does not require significant expenses, it would be best to own the best tools among the bunch.
