Why is Exercise So Important to the Body?

  •      If you are on a weight loss journey, combine fitness, and clean eating to achieve and maintain a healthy weight.
  •    Did you know that by exercising, you help your body to flush out the bacterial build up in your airways and lungs?
  •    Exercise can help you achieve a restful state needed for deep sleep.

    •     The human body functions best when one has an active lifestyle.For, our muscles grow or wear out depending on the amount of physical activity we have.Think about it.A bedridden patient needs to engage in some routine exercise once in a while.Otherwise, after some time, his body parts may grow stiff, interfering with his motor functions.Then, what are the other reasons to incorporate regular exercise routines in our lifestyles?

        1.Improves Flexibility

       A healthy body comprises joints and muscles that stretch easily.That way, has better muscle coordination, good posture, and is less prone to injuries as a result of a rigid body.Further, a flexible body can recover faster from strenuous activity or injury.Hence, regular exercise in gymnastics or martial arts involves most body muscles.This way, you flex more muscles than you would have if you were in a seated position each day.

       2.Keeps Body Weight Under Control

       Regular exercise stimulates our metabolism rate.In turn, our bodies burn more fat and calories when we are active.In contrast, someone who lives a sedentary lifestyle will find it hard to keep off the extra pounds.Then, if you are on a weight loss journey, combine fitness, and clean eating to achieve and maintain a healthy weight.

       3.Boosts Our Immunity

       Did you know that by exercising, you help your body to flush out the bacterial build up in your airways and lungs?Hence, you can fight a common cold or flu by taking adequate fruits and soups, plus some form of exercise during the day.Second, as you exercise, you provoke your white blood cells to circulate more often.This way, they pick up on any infection and fight it before it gets out of hand.

        4.Slows Down the Aging Process

       Anyone who incorporates a regular exercise routine in his lifestyle can defy the aging process.Indeed, through exercise, the body builds and repairs more muscles, boosts immunity, and cuts down on the harmful cholesterol levels.All these processes work towards slowing down the onset of poor health associated with aging.Hence, an active person will have fewer chances of developing osteoporosis, high blood pressure, diabetes, and heart attack.

       5.Helps to Manage Stress

       If you are going through a rough patch in your life, regular exercise can help you remain focused on finding the solution out of the fix.For often, stress can overwhelm one to the point of becoming irrational.And, when mood swings set in, we tend to wallow in a pity party, blaming ourselves for the misfortunes.But when you go out for a nature walk, the trees around you release phytoncides, which help us to get rid of such negative energy.

       6.Improves Sleeping Patterns

       It is a proven fact; exercise can help you achieve a restful state needed for deep sleep.For physical training puts a check on your cardiac health, stress, and anxiety levels.Besides, when you engage in rigorous activities like swimming, your body gets exhausted.In turn, when you go to sleep, you fall asleep faster and maintain deep sleep for longer.

       Conclusion

       Other benefits of exercise include achieving a healthy mind, building endurance, and improving our self-esteem.Then, if you are yet to incorporate exercise in your daily routines, start by light activities indoors.Then, slowly shift to HIITs, setting achievable fitness targets.The bottom line is; you should strive to be active at all times.
