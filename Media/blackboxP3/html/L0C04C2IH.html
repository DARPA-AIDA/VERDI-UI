FALSE: The Washington Post published a story saying that COVID-19 patient zero was an employee from a virology institute in Wuhan.

    Explanation: The Washington Post did publish an article talking about safety concerns about this lab, citing American intelligence sources, but never stated that the 'patient zero' was an employee.The Washington Post fact-checking service itself classified this information as false.
