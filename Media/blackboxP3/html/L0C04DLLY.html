       The aspect of intellectual property does not preclude any physical being, it is generally convergent in an idea and the subsequent inclusion into any physical product.In the form of logos, trademarks, and other IPs, they can become associated with the human psyche and often become the cause of the maintenance of the success of that brand over other related products.
